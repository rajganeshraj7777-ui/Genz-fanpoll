Privacy Policy

We store vote count using Firebase database. We use localStorage to prevent multiple votes from the same device.

When you post a comment, your name and comment will be stored in the database and shown publicly. Do not share personal information in comments.

Third Party Services

We use Firebase for storing votes and comments.
